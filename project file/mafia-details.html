Mafia III

Description

The new release of the cult series about the everyday life of organized crime. For the first time players get a chance to create your own clan, to subdue the city and embody the grandiose plan of revenge.

In 1968, after years in Vietnam, Lincoln Clay knows this truth: Family isn't who you're born with, it's who you are willing to die for. Now back home, in a new New Orleans, Lincoln is dedicated to rebuilding his life, but established ties keep him from exiting the city. It'll take more than a few friends to make it in this new world.
